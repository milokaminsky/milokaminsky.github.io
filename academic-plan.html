Athletic Plan

Personal & Technical Skills

  • Interview Skills

  • Public Speaking
  • Hobbies

Finance

We will work with the client to assist in clarifying their vision of a college match which includes an overarching understanding of academic, environmental and financial needs.

  • Level of Academic Challenge
  • Location
  • Size of College/University
  • Majors/minors or concentrations
  • College Environment
  • Type of Housing
  • Major/Minor
  • Desired Activities
  • Support Services

Report

This discussion will help define the client's college preference with regard to several key factors. Our final report will present an accurate, clear and focused College Portfolio which will take into account:

  • College List
  • A full discussion of each choice; its merits, desirability, and vital statistics.
  • To assist in creating focus, career building and goal setting
  • Expected scholarships
  • A discussion of scholarships particular to a given student and university/college.
  • Expected federal and state aid
  • Most people can receive at least some federal and state aid. The report will define and facilitate the acquisition of these funds.
  • In addition, we will provide suggestions for developing the student resume, enhancing the High School Transcript, assistance in negotiating an equitable financial package, advice on letters of recommendations, availability of outside scholarships, and advice on enhancing the effectiveness of the college tour
