Academic Plan

Our initial meeting will include a comprehensive assessment of the client. We will compile, appraise, and critique a wide range of academic, social and personal interests.

Academic Strengths

  • Evaluate Standardized Tests (SAT, ACT, TELSOL)
  • Interpret HS Transcript
  • Identify Awards/Honors
  • Appraise Student Resume
  • Assist in Acquiring Letters of Recommendation

Extracirricular Pursuits

  • Volunteer Experience
  • Work History
  • Sports Participation
  • Travel
  • Leadership Activities

Personal & Technical Skills

  • Musical Talent
  • Computer Based Acumen
  • Interview Skills
  • Artistic Expression and Portfolios
  • Public Speaking
  • Hobbies

Finance

Athletic Plan

We analyze your tools and skills with one of our professional talent evaluators (including the scout who discovered and signed Mike Trout). Provide fair and unbiased analysis of your athletic tools and skills by highly respected industry professionals Provide recommendations where you can play in college When your video analysis is complete, we will work with you to determine the athletic level of collegiate placement that we are comfortable contacting and assisting. You must be willing to hear an honest assessment of your current and projected skills. At no point will we misrepresent you to programs we do not feel are the right fit and we will not place our own financial gain above our player evaluation integrity. We will assist with the option of providing Collegiate Coaches everything you academically and financially qualify for at their specific school, assisting the coaches and making the decision process easier for everyone involved. You will receive regular updates, copies of communication and any information relevant to the student and family throughout the process. (If applicable) Note: If you are providing your own video, we will do the best we can but please bear in mind we can only work within the constraints of what you send.

Report

This discussion will help define the client's college preference with regard to several key factors. Our final report will present an accurate, clear and focused College Portfolio which will take into account:

  • College List
  • A full discussion of each choice; its merits, desirability, and vital statistics.
  • To assist in creating focus, career building and goal setting
  • Expected scholarships
  • A discussion of scholarships particular to a given student and university/college.
  • Expected federal and state aid
  • Most people can receive at least some federal and state aid. The report will define and facilitate the acquisition of these funds.
  • In addition, we will provide suggestions for developing the student resume, enhancing the High School Transcript, assistance in negotiating an equitable financial package, advice on letters of recommendations, availability of outside scholarships, and advice on enhancing the effectiveness of the college tour
