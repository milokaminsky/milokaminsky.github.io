About Us

Selecting a college is time consuming, pressure-filled, and fraught with confusion. For anyone looking for top academic options or student athletes seeking to continue their career at the collegiate level, the process is even more difficult.

Elite Guidance has a fresh, simple and genuine approach to the collegiate and athletic process. We provide the top resources and most accurate information to allow every student to find the best collegiate environment for their situation. Elite Guidance features supremely qualified, passionate individuals at the peak of their fields. We brought them together to guide and support each client in their unique goals. We don't utilize fear, we use facts.

Whether working with our staff or attending an Elite Guidance camp, instructional clinic or tournament, you can be assured you will have the optimal environment for success. We will be guiding you every step of the way through your personal collegiate journey. The rest, as it is said, is up to you.
