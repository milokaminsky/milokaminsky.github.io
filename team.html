Our Staff

Vincent Lumetta

Vincent Lumetta attended St John's University and got his Masters in Education with a specialty in Counseling from Hofstra. During his 31 year career with the Department of Education, 7 years of which were teaching and 24 years as a guidance counselor, he has won Counselor of the Year award 4 times. Throughout his career, he has been called to work in as many as 5 schools at the same time while helping hundreds of students attend hundreds of colleges with financial aid. Currently, he works as a Guidance and College Counselor with World Journalism Preparatory School where he has worked to get 3+ million in financial aid annually for the 75-85 students. He also works closely with the NCAA to make sure World Journalism Preparatory School is adhering to all the standards the NCAA sets to have student-athletes eligible to play collegiate sports. Vincent is a member of National Association of College Counselors, New York State Counseling Association and the National Counselors Association. In addition, he also has a certificate in teaching and counseling.

Ian Millman

Ian is an Associate Scout for the Atlanta Braves. Previously he held the same position for the Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim, Pittsburgh Pirates and the Toronto Blue Jays. From 2008-2010 he was Head Coach at Francis Lewis HS, Queens NY and has the highest winning percentage (.790) in school history. In 2008 Ian coached Francis Lewis to their second division championship in 22 years. In 2009, he coached Francis Lewis to its first back-to-back division championships in school history. Former RHP who played two seasons professionally. President, Co-Founder New York Nine

Milo Kaminsky

Milo graduated Franklin & Marshall College with a degree in Economics. He worked at Major League Baseball Advanced Media as an analyst of Major League Baseball Umpires Strike Zones and ran the Pitch FX (records both velocity and movement) system at both Yankee Stadium and CitiField. In addition, he scored Minor Leagues games for milb.com.
